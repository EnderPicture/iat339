Branding ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Industrial electronic power converters/transformers. They can also do custom designs per customer's request.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Big and small companies' product procurement center's workers

Persona

John Wakoski, who is the head of product procurement for 3M commercial products, wants to buy some DC to DC power converters to power their new CCD cameras. The Cameras run off of 12v power but the powerlines in the factories all run at 24V. He wishes to get a good quality product that will last a long time for a reasonable price and be environmentally conscious.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Good quality reliable parts and environmental construction
